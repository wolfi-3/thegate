The First Hello

The Quiet Moments

The Hard Days

This room isn't dark — it's honest. Love isn't only the beautiful parts. It's also showing up on the days that are hard, choosing each other even when it's easier not to. And you have. Every time.

"The strongest love isn't the one that never bends — it's the one that bends and stays."

The Adventures

Every place we've been, every wrong turn that became the best part of the trip, every time we got lost and somehow it was perfect — you make the whole world feel like an adventure worth having.

The Things I Admire

Your kindness that you don't even notice you're giving. The way you think about things. Your laugh. Your stubbornness (yes, even that). The way you love the people you love. I could keep this door open forever.

"I didn't fall in love with one thing. I fell in love with a thousand small ones."

VII

The Future I Want

I don't need to know every detail. I just know I want you in it. More mornings. More trips. More ordinary Tuesdays that end up being the ones we remember. Whatever it looks like — I want it with you.

"The best futures are the ones you don't have to plan alone."

VIII

The Reason for All of This

You made it through every door. But the truth is — there was never really an escape. Because with you, I don't want to escape. I want to stay, again and again, in every room, in every chapter, in every ordinary and extraordinary moment.

I love you. That's the whole secret.
